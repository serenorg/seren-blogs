## What Makes Networking Better Than Applying Online?

Before we dive into how the skill works, let's understand **why networking crushes traditional applications**:

### The Double-Tap Strategy

1. **Apply via ATS** (Applicant Tracking System) â†’ Get confirmation ID
2. **Email hiring manager directly** â†’ Reference your application ID
3. **Result**: Maximum visibilityâ€”HR sees your application AND the hiring manager knows to look for it

Most candidates do step 1 only. A few do step 2 manually (and burn out after 5 companies). Seren automates both at scale.

### The Numbers Don't Lie

- **Cold applications**: ~2% response rate
- **Networking outreach**: ~20-30% response rate
- **Double-tap (both)**: ~40%+ response rate

Why? Because hiring managers get 10 networking emails per week but HR gets 500+ applications per day. **You need to stand out where there's less noise.**

### Phase-by-Phase Breakdown

**Phase 0: Profile Extraction**
Upload your resume (PDF) and LinkedIn data export. The AI extracts your skills, experience, target roles, and location preferences into a structured profile.

**Phase 1: Company Discovery**
Using AlphaGrowth's database, Seren finds 50 companies matching your criteria (industry, location, funding stage, size). This isn't generic job board dataâ€”these are high-growth startups and scale-ups actively hiring.

**Phase 2: Company Research**
The AI researches the top 20 companies using Perplexity (real-time web search) and Exa (deep content indexing). It identifies recent funding, product launches, hiring priorities, and cultural signalsâ€”everything you'd manually research on Crunchbase, LinkedIn, and company blogs.

**Phase 3: Contact Discovery**
Apollo.io finds 100+ hiring managers, VPs of Engineering, and team leads at your top 10 companies. You get verified emails, LinkedIn profiles, and social context (shared connections, common interests).

**Phase 4: Event Discovery**
Seren searches for networking events, conferences, and meetups in your industry and location. These become conversational hooks for your outreach emails ("I saw you're speaking at AI Summit next month...").

**Phase 5a: Email Verification**
AlphaGrowth verifies email deliverability to ensure your outreach doesn't bounce (nothing kills credibility like a bounced email).

**Phase 5b: Outreach Generation**
GPT-5.2 generates 3 hyper-personalized emails that:
- Reference your actual background and skills
- Mention the company's recent news or priorities
- Include shared connections or upcoming events
- Ask for a 15-minute informational chat (low-friction ask)

**Phase 6: Application Tracking**
A local SQLite database tracks every company, contact, outreach email, and application. No more spreadsheet chaos.

**Phase 7: Auto-Apply (Optional)**
For companies where you've networked, Seren can auto-apply to their ATS jobs (Greenhouse, Lever) using Playwright automation. It generates tailored resumes per role, solves CAPTCHAs, and captures confirmation IDsâ€”which you reference in your follow-up email to the hiring manager.

## When NOT to Use This Skill

**Be honest with yourself:**

1. **You're not serious about the job search** - This automates execution, not decision-making. You still need to define your target role, industries, and review the results.

2. **You want to spam 500 companies** - The skill is designed for quality over quantity. 50 companies, deep research, targeted outreach. Not mass spam.

3. **You're applying for roles requiring government clearance or highly regulated industries** - Networking works, but these industries have strict application processes.

4. **You have zero LinkedIn presence** - The skill needs your LinkedIn data export to build context. If you've never used LinkedIn, start there first.

## Getting Started: Your First Campaign

Here's how to run your first job search campaign in Seren:

### Step 1: Download Your LinkedIn Data

1. Go to [linkedin.com/mypreferences/d/download-my-data](https://linkedin.com/mypreferences/d/download-my-data)
2. Select "Download larger data archive"
3. Request archive (takes 10-15 minutes)
4. Download the ZIP file when ready

### Step 2: Set Up Seren

1. Download [Seren Desktop](https://serendb.com)
2. Create account and add payment method
3. Deposit $60 in SerenBucks (covers full campaign + buffer)

### Step 3: Run the Job Seeker Skill

```bash
# Phase 0: Extract your profile
python agent.py extract-profile \
  --resume resume.pdf \
  --linkedin-export linkedin_export.zip \
  --output profile.json

# Phase 1: Discover companies
python agent.py discover \
  --profile profile.json \
  --role "Senior ML Engineer" \
  --industry "Artificial Intelligence" \
  --location "San Francisco" \
  --limit 50 \
  --output companies.json

# Phase 2: Research top companies
python agent.py research \
  --companies companies.json \
  --limit 20 \
  --output research.json

# Phase 3: Find hiring managers
python agent.py find-contacts \
  --companies research.json \
  --titles "Engineering Manager,VP Engineering" \
  --limit 10 \
  --output contacts.json

# Phase 4: Discover events
python agent.py discover-events \
  --location "San Francisco" \
  --industry "AI,Machine Learning" \
  --output events.json

# Phase 5: Generate outreach
python agent.py generate-outreach \
  --contacts contacts.json \
  --profile profile.json \
  --events events.json \
  --limit 3 \
  --output outreach.json
```

**Total time**: 30 minutes (most of it waiting for API calls)
**Total cost**: $20.30

### Step 4: Review & Send

Seren generates 3 personalized emails in `outreach.json`. Review them, make any tweaks, and send via your preferred email client.

**Pro tip**: Send from your personal email, not a work email. Use a tool like Mailshake or Lemlist for tracking opens/clicks.
